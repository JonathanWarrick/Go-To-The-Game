Welcome to Go To The Game!

Go To The Game! shows ticket pricing information for upcoming home games for your favorite team.

The higher line shows the average price per game on SeatGeek.com. The lower line shows the weighted average price per game, using the combined win percentage of each team to weight the relative value in team quality per dollar spent.

Go To The Game! is powered by SeatGeek, SportsData, and Chartist.js!
